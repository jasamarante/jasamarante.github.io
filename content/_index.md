## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral researcher, currently based at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](http://astro.sjtu.edu.cn/en), working with Professor [Li Zhao-Yu](http://astro.sjtu.edu.cn/en/staff/researcher/109-)’s dynamic group. I study the structure and evolution of the Milky Way with SPH+N body simulations and observational data. Check [my ADS library](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/public-libraries/cmbkFq9KQW22BsUDu-STUA) for a complete list of my publications, which includes papers I co-authored.

If you are an undergrad student looking for a short-term project, feel free to contact me! Se você é um estudante de graduação e está procurando um projeto de iniciação científica, fique a vontade para entrar em contato!
